Importance of Nutrition in Gym

Balanced Diet

Nutrition plays a vital role in achieving fitness goals. Consuming a balanced diet rich in protein, carbohydrates, and healthy fats fuels the body for workouts and aids in muscle recovery.

Moreover, staying hydrated and incorporating fruits and vegetables into your diet provide essential vitamins and minerals necessary for overall health.
